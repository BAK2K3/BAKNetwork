What's a CNN?
A CNN is a class of networks commonly used for analyzing image data!
Convolutional Neural Networks contain 3-dimensional layers (width, height, and depth),
and use filters to perform 'feature extraction' on images, ultimately assigning a probability
to the possible classifications being trained.
