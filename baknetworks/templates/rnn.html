What's an RNN?
An RNN uses internal memory states to "remember" sequences of information.
From training an RNN on huge corpuses of text (such as shakespears texts, or War and Pace by Tolstoy), RNN's have the ability to predict subsequent information from previous sequences of information.
The below implementations of RNNs are capable of producing text on a letter by letter basis!
