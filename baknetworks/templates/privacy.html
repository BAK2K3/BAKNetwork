Privacy Notice

Introduction Privacy is extremely important and this page is to outline how your privacy is protected when using BAKNet.
Who I am I am an independent developer, and this portfolio is for demonstrative purposes only.
What information do I collect? If you are not logging in, then no information is collected. If you are logging in, then your Email address and name will be stored in a database, along with a unique access "token".
How do I use your personal information? This information is used for website access, and for utilising the deploy models only. This information is not analysed or distributed.
What legal basis do I have for prociessing your personal data? By logging into this website, you are providing your consent for this data to be stored. However, you can revoke this consent from your google account at any time.
When do I share personal data? The only information that is shared is your name, and that is when leaving comments or storing model history. These comments and histories can be deleted at any time, and can only be viewed by others who have logged in.
Where do I store and process personal data? This information is stored in a secured database, which cannot be accessed by other users.
How do I secure personal data? Your data is secured via Heroku's server, and will periodically be maintained/cleared on a monthly basis.
How long do I keep your personal data for? The databases containing personal information will be cleared once a month.
Your rights in relation to personal data Under GDPR, you have the right to all data retained in the database including:
Access to personal information, correction and deletion, withdrawal or consent, data portability, restriction of processing and objection, and loging a complaint with the Information Commissioner's Office. You can exercise any of your rights at any time by emailing benjamin.a.kavanagh@gmail.com
Use of automated decision-making and profiling No automated decision making or profiling is utilised.
How to contact me? You can contact me on benjamin.a.kavanagh@gmail.com
